<h2>The Box</h2>


<h2>Description:</h2>

This is a small game made by me on a very boring day. Rules are simple: Collect the violet coins, Avoid the blue boxes, and reach the white box.

<h2>Controls:</h2>
<ul>
<li>Arrow Keys to move</li>
</ul>
